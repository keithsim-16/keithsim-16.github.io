HOW WE STARTED

TOTEM. is inspired by the core material used to build the buildings around us – Concrete. We found it interesting to bring the city back into the living room and each of our products are reminiscent of the city and the opportunities that exist out there.

All TOTEM. products are unique and handmade with love. The use of concrete means that there are natural variations in surface texture and colour within each item. But we believe these properties are what gave each TOTEM. product the charming rustic look that is so unique and appealing.
